What to Visit

Take a short trip to nearby towns or natural attractions and experience more.
Rogoznica
~8 km · 10 min

A relaxed seaside town with a lively marina, promenade, and crystal clear sea, perfect for evening walks and dining by the water.

Primošten
~17 km · 25 min

A picturesque old town on a small peninsula, famous for its beaches, stone streets, and stunning sunsets.

Trogir
~25 km · 30 min

A UNESCO-listed historic town full of narrow streets, beautiful architecture, and vibrant atmosphere.

Split
~50 km · 1 h

A vibrant coastal city where you can explore Diocletian’s Palace, shops, and nightlife.

National park Krka
~50 km · 1 h

A beautiful national park known for waterfalls, wooden walkways, and nature-perfect for a day trip.
