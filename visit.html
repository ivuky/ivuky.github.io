Nearby Destinations

From hidden coves to UNESCO towns. Six stunning places you can reach in under an hour from Lagoonica.

Rogoznica

Distance & time: 8 km · 10 min

Description: Rogoznica is a peaceful fishing village built around a deep, natural bay. Enjoy the crystal‑clear sea, the famous Dragon’s Eye Lake (a karst spring), and waterfront promenades with authentic konobas.

Highlights: Dragon’s Eye Lake, beaches (Šepurine, Crljina), local seafood restaurants.

Primošten

Distance & time: 17 km · 25 min

Description: Primošten is a charming peninsula known for its vineyards that climb the hills, narrow stone streets, and beautiful pebble beaches. Climb to the Church of St. George for a postcard view.

Highlights: Old town, beach Raduča, wine tasting in the surrounding hills.

Trogir

Distance & time: 25 km · 30 min

Description: Trogir is a UNESCO World Heritage site – a beautifully preserved medieval town on a small island. Walk the Romanesque, Renaissance, and Baroque streets, visit the Cathedral of St. Lawrence, and enjoy the lively waterfront.

Highlights: Kamerlengo Castle, Cathedral, narrow streets, boat tours to nearby islands.

Split

Distance & time: 50 km · 1 h

Description: Split is the largest coastal city in Dalmatia, built around the ancient Diocletian’s Palace (another UNESCO site). Stroll the Riva promenade, explore the palace’s basements, and climb the bell tower for an amazing view.

Highlights: Diocletian’s Palace, Riva, Marjan Hill, bustling markets, nightlife.

National park Krka

Distance & time: 50 km · 1 h

Description: Krka is a stunning national park famous for its series of waterfalls, most notably Skradinski Buk. Walk the wooden pathways, take a boat ride to Visovac Island, and swim in designated areas (unlike Plitvice).

Highlights: Skradinski Buk waterfall, Roski Slap, boat to Visovac monastery, hiking trails.

Šibenik

Distance & time: 40 km · 50 min

Description: Šibenik is a historic town built on a hill, with the magnificent Cathedral of St. James (UNESCO) made entirely of stone. Explore the narrow alleys, the fortress of St. Michael, and the nearby St. Nicholas Fortress.

Highlights: St. James Cathedral, St. Michael’s Fortress, coastal promenade, boat to the Kornati Islands.
