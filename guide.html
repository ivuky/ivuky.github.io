Guest Guide – Your Stay at Lagoonica

Everything you need for a smooth arrival, a comfortable stay, and a stress‑free departure.
Before You Arrive

Booking confirmation: After the reservation fee is received, you'll receive a booking confirmation on your email.

Receipt: After full payment is completed, you'll receive a booking receipt on your email.

Payment & cancellation deadlines: Please respect deadlines so that your reservation does not end up being cancelled. 

Arrival & Parking

Parking: Free private parking is available right next to the building. A reserved spot is marked with “Lagoonica”.

Key collection: We’ll greet you in person. Please send us your estimated arrival time at least 1 hour in advance. If we're unable to greet you, please refer to "Entering the apartment" section below on how to enter your apartment.

Entering the Apartment

Key box code: You’ll receive a unique code 24 hours before arrival to access the key from the secure key box next to apartment entrance.

Internet & Wi‑Fi: Network name and password will be displayed in a sign holder on kitchen table with scannable QR code.

Air conditioning: Remote control is on the bedroom dresser. For energy savings, please turn off AC when windows are open.

During Your Stay

Parties & events: You're not allowed to organize parties or events for people who are not registered guests of Lagoonica.

Smoking and narcotics: Smoking indoors and the use of narcotics on the premises are strictly prohibited.

Quiet hours: Please respect neighbors, quiet hours are between 23:00 and 07:00.

Maintenance: Kindly keep the apartment clean and tidy during your stay. Garbage bin is in the courtyard.

Damages: Any damages should be reported immediately.

Laundry: Foldable drying rack is on the balcony. Please hang laundry only on the rack – not on the balcony railing.

BBQ area: You’re welcome to use the garden barbecue. Please clean the grill after use.

Lost & found: Contact us immediately. We’ll hold any forgotten items for 30 days.

Check‑out & Before Leaving

Check‑out time: 10:00 (unless otherwise agreed).

What to do:
- Take out all trash to the outside bins.
- Place used towels in the bathroom hamper.
- Wash and put away dishes, or run the dishwasher.
- Turn off all lights, air conditioning, and appliances.
- Close all windows and doors.
- Leave the apartment key on the kitchen table, no need for locking the apartment.

Final inspection: We’ll do a quick walkthrough after you leave.
